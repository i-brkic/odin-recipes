Pasta Pomodoro

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta of choice
  • 2 garlics neatly chopped
  • 500ml tomato sauce
  • 100g cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Cook water until it simmers
  2. Put pasta in it for about 10 minutes
  3. After pasta is al dente, drain it and put in the bowl for eating
  4. Add cheese and spices.
  5. Enjoy!
